Apply for help to buy productivity items

Use this service to apply for help to buy farming productivity items.

Application closing date: 17 April 2025, midday

Before you apply

You must have registered with the Rural Payments service to apply.

You can apply for someone else if they've given you permission to apply on their behalf in the Rural Payments service.

You can check the productivity items and specification list to see which items you can apply for.

What you’ll need

If you apply for more than £25,000, you’ll need:

  • evidence to show you can cover the total cost - for example, evidence of a personal or private loan, or a bank or building society statement, email or letter
  • financial accounts for the most recent 3 years of trading
  • your latest tax returns, management accounts or a statement of income and expenses from an accountant (if you’re a new business or self-employed)
