Apply for a grant to buy slurry stores, covers and items

Use this service to apply for a grant to buy:

  • slurry stores
  • slurry covers
  • slurry items and accessories

You’re eligible if your organisation is based in England, your farming system already produces slurry, and you farm pigs, beef or dairy cattle.

Application closing date: 17 April 2025, midday

Before you apply

You must have registered with the Rural Payments service to apply.

You can apply for someone else if they've given you permission to apply on their behalf in the Rural Payments service.

You can check the slurry grant guidance to see which stores, covers and items you can apply for and how much you could get.

What you’ll need

You’ll need:

  • a copy of your full planning permission for the slurry storage
  • details of your current and planned slurry stores, including capacity
  • full accounts for the last 3 years, or your latest tax returns or management accounts if you’ve been trading for less than 3 years
  • project funding evidence
  • slurry storage design declaration form and drawings

If applicable to your organisation and project, you’ll also need:

  • an Environmental Permitting Regulations permit variation document
  • slurry acidification equipment and techniques evidence
  • tenancy agreement information and landlord consent confirmation
