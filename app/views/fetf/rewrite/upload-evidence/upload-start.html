Financial evidence

As you're applying for more than £25,000, you’ll need to upload 2 types of evidence so we can do financial checks.

1. Evidence you can cover the total item costs

We’ll ask you to upload evidence that shows you can cover the cost of items until the grant is claimed.

You can use a:

  • bank or building society statement
  • bank or building society email or letter
  • evidence of a personal or private loan

2. Evidence the organisation is financially viable

We’ll then ask you to upload evidence to show the organisation who will receive the grant is financially viable.

You can use a:

  • full financial accounts for the last 3 years of trading
  • your latest tax returns, management accounts or a statement of income and expenses from an accountant (if you’re a new business or self-employed)
