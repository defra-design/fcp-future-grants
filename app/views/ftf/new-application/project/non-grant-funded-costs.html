Estimated non grant-funded project costs

The following costs are not eligible for grant funding (this list is not exhaustive):

Agricultural and consumable costs

  1. Agricultural production rights and payment entitlements costs
  2. Ongoing ‘consumable’ costs (like poultry feed).

Equipment and items

  1. Egg quality, packing or processing equipment.
  2. Items not directly associated with the funded building.
  3. Moveable items not in the minimum design specification.
  4. Standard agricultural equipment and inputs.
  5. Work involving, or items located within, the range (like pond removal, tree planting or artificial shelters).

Facilities and housing

  1. Manure storage facilities.
  2. Mobile housing.
  3. Non-permanent structure not included in the minimum design specification.

Systems

  1. Automated vehicle washing systems.
  2. Combi-cage systems.
  3. High-pressure cooling (misting) systems.
  4. Solar PV systems for installation on the range, or on roofs that face only north or are fully shaded.
