Non grant-funded costs

There are some costs that you cannot claim funding for.

Costs the grant cannot pay for.

The following costs are not eligible for grant funding (this list is not exhaustive):

  • agricultural production rights and payment entitlements costs
  • ongoing ‘consumable’ costs (such as wood shavings or poultry feed)

Equipment and items

  • equipment relating to egg quality, packing or processing
  • items not directly associated with the funded building
  • moveable items not in the minimum design specification
  • standard agricultural equipment and inputs (like farm vehicles and poultry)
  • work involving, or items located within, the range (like pond removal, tree planting or artificial shelters)

Facilities and housing

  • manure storage facilities
  • mobile housing
  • non-permanent structure not included in the minimum design specification

Systems

  • automated vehicle washing systems
  • combi-cage systems
  • high-pressure cooling (misting) systems
  • solar PV systems for installation on the range, or on roofs that face only north or are fully shaded
