Check ventilation features

The housing must have all the following:

  1. Air speed of 1 metre per second over the birds.
  2. Alarm system to detect and commute excessive high or low temperatures and system failure, with a power supply independent of mains electricity.
  3. Control system to automatically measure and record daily temperature, humidity and CO2 levels.
  4. Emergency power supply.
  5. Maximum ventilation rate of 10,800m3 per hour per 1000 birds (up to 2.5 kg body mass).
  6. Mechanical ventilation system.

Confirm the project will have these features
