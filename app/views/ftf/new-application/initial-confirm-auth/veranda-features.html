Check veranda features

You only need to add a veranda if you have the outside space for it. The veranda must either be:

  • 4 metres wide along the length of the housing area
  • 30% of the indoor housing area footprint

Veranda

If you add a veranda, it must have all the following:

  1. Dimmable LED lighting system (0 lux to 60 lux).
  2. Mesh roller screen running under the roof length, that fits securely against the wall when you roll it down.
  3. Perimeter wall at least 1m high, with biosecure entrance for cleaning access.
  4. Solid concrete floor.
  5. Waterproof insulated roof.

Pop holes

The veranda also meet all the pop hole requirements:

  1. All pop holes bases are less than 30cm from floor level, or have access ramps as wide as the pop holes.
  2. Closable pop holes in the perimeter wall, unless the veranda is part of an indoor barn system.
  3. Internal access along the hen house wall length through closable pop holes are at least 35cm high and 40cm wide.
  4. No perches in front of pop holes.
  5. Width of pop hole openings along the internal hen house wall length adds up to a total of 2 metres for every 1,000 hens in the building.

Confirm the project will have these features

By continuing, you confirm the project will have all these features.

If your project does not have all these features, you will not be eligible for this grant.
