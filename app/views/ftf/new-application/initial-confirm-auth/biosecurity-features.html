Housing

The housing must have all the following:

  1. Continuous concrete apron surrounding it, unless the birds need a gap to directly access a range.
  2. Designated on-site area to wash and disinfect vehicles with a concrete surface at least 3 metres wide, and soiled water drainage system and storage tanks.
  3. Direct external access to the egg store (must be separate from main entrance lobby and connected changing area).

Changing area

It must also have a biosecurity changing area at each external pedestrian access point. Each area must have all the following:

  1. Changing facilities, with step-over barrier between outer and inner areas.
  2. Inner area: footbath and indoor clothes and boots storage.
  3. Outer area: handwashing facilities with running water and outdoor clothes and boots storage.

Confirm the project will have these features

By continuing, you confirm the project will have all these features.

If your project does not have all these features, you will not be eligible for this grant.
