Housing

The housing must have all the following:

  1. Catch trays under chimneys and roof-mounted vents.
  2. Fixed structure with a solid concrete floor.
  3. Full wall and roof insulation.
  4. Mesh capping applied to any roof or wall inlets and outlets (using a mesh hole of 25 millimetres or less)
  5. Watertight roof and walls.

House lighting system

The house lighting system must have all the following:

  1. Full coverage of exposed floor-litter (scratch) area .
  2. Non-flicker LED light with colour temperature between 2,700 and 4,000 Kelvin.
  3. Red light option.
  4. Zonal dimming capacity (0 to 60 lux). 

Confirm the project will have these features

By continuing, you confirm the project will have all these features.

If your project does not have all these features, you will not be eligible for this grant.
