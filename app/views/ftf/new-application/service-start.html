Apply for a hen housing grant

Apply for funding to upgrade or replace hen or pullet housing, or to add a veranda.

To apply, you must:

  • be a registered keeper of at least 1000 laying hens or pullets
  • have housed at least 1000 laying hens or pullets on your farm for the last 6 months
  • have not started project work
  • have planning permission by the grant application deadline, or permitted development rights
  • own the land youâ€™re building on, or have a tenancy agreement for at least 5 years after the project is complete
This grant is for projects in England only.

What you'll need

You must have registered with the Rural Payments service to apply.

You'll need:

  • project specifications
  • business details
  • existing hen housing details
  • estimated project costs

The Rural Payments Agency will use the information you provide to decide whether to award you grant funding. This includes due diligence and fraud prevention checks.
